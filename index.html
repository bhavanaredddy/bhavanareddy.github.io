Research
Publication
📑 IntelliPay: Intelligent Forecasting of Payment Due Dates Using Machine Learning
Overview

Production-ready, end-to-end ML system for predicting invoice payment delays in financial and enterprise platforms. Processes payment, invoice, and customer behavior data through structured pipelines with cleaning, validation, and feature engineering.

Methodology

Applied EDA and statistical techniques to identify key risk indicators including payment frequency, delay history, and billing cycle trends. Trained and compared Logistic Regression, Decision Trees, Random Forest, Gradient Boosting, and XGBoost — evaluated on accuracy, precision, recall, F1-score, and ROC-AUC.

Results

XGBoost achieved MAE 3.52 days, RMSE 5.47, and R² of 0.89 with K-Fold validation. Engineered aging buckets (0–15, 16–30, 31–45, 46–60, 60+ days) and risk profiles using Min-Max and Standard Scaling. Deployed secure web dashboard with CAPTCHA login for data upload and prediction export as Excel files.

Impact

Enables proactive flagging of high-risk payments before due dates, improving cash-flow forecasting, reducing operational risk, and supporting automated reminder and intervention strategies. Scalable and production-ready for real-world financial platforms.
